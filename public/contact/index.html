Do you want to become a member of LuxDoc?

As a LuxDoc member, you support the activities of LuxDoc on all levels, from operating costs to events. Furthermore, you will get some special discounts on our various events. Finally, membership allows you to vote at the general assembly and to join the committee and become active.

If you wish to become a member of LuxDoc, please fill in the form below.
